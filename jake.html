Bio

Jake supports bargaining unit employees across Los Padres National Forest with representation in investigatory and disciplinary matters, grievance guidance, working condition disputes, and policy interpretation.

His approach emphasizes clear timelines, accurate documentation, and consistent application of the Master Agreement, Forest Service directives, and applicable federal regulations.
