Bio

Ella supports bargaining unit employees across Los Padres National Forest with representation in investigatory and disciplinary matters, grievance guidance, working condition disputes, and policy interpretation.

Her approach emphasizes clear timelines, accurate documentation, and consistent application of the Master Agreement, Forest Service directives, and applicable federal regulations.

Use the contact panel to reach Ella directly, or return to the main site to submit an intake form or schedule an appointment.
